CarryOnStriving
  • Our Vision

We believe all people regardless of age should be given the opportunity to pursue their dreams and give back to their community

CarryOnStriving is an idea was originated from UCL Disrupt, a business competition within UCL, which our goal is to provide an innovative service in response to the global phenomenon of increasing life expectancy, encouraging a socially active and financially sustainable retirement.

We have built an intergenerational community to help the elderly develop their skills and interests, turning them into projects or micro-businesses which can be shared and recognised by their community. Allowing the younger generation to share their passion, energy and digitally savvy nature among the older community.
